Rules

Rate yourself according to each statement describing your character. The fruit in which you score the lowest you may need to consider starting your Christ coaching sessions their first and practicing repentance and growth in this area.

  • A” Agree (3 - points) = “I practice this regularly. I can teach a class and help others in this area.”
  • “S” Somewhat agree (2 - points) = “I generally walk in this character trait unless I am overwhelmed or bothered.”
  • “N”= Needs improvement (0 - points) = “I really need help in this area. Sometimes I even feel like I am the way I am.” We are to continually be conscious to pray, inspired by the life Christ and prompted to become more like Him.
